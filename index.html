About DET 3

DET 3 is a group of 24 young Black men who have committed to a 15-month journey of growth. We’re learning leadership, programming, entrepreneurship, and how to create real change in our communities. Throughout this program, we support each other, share knowledge, and build lasting relationships.

We believe in the power of Ubuntu—"I am because we are." Our success is built on working together and lifting each other up. As we develop our skills, we also strengthen our bond as brothers.

This isn’t just about learning skills; it’s about making an impact. We’re here to build a legacy that will change the future for ourselves and our communities.
